## 1. Getting Started

### System Requirements

#### Desktop Requirements
- **Browser**: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- **Screen Resolution**: 1024x768 minimum
- **JavaScript**: Enabled
- **Cookies**: Enabled
- **Internet Connection**: Broadband recommended

#### Mobile Requirements
- **Operating System**: iOS 12+, Android 8+
- **Browser**: Safari 12+, Chrome Mobile 90+
- **Screen Size**: 320px minimum width
- **Storage**: 50MB available space
- **Internet Connection**: 4G/LTE or WiFi

### Account Registration

#### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Visit Registration Page**
   - Navigate to https://vebstore.com/register
   - Click "Create Account" button

2. **Fill Registration Form**
   ```
   First Name: [Enter your first name]
   Last Name: [Enter your last name]
   Email: [Enter your email address]
   Phone: [Enter your phone number]
   Password: [Create a strong password]
   Confirm Password: [Re-enter password]
   ```

3. **Password Requirements**
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter
   - At least one lowercase letter
   - At least one number
   - At least one special character

4. **Verify Information**
   - Review all entered information
   - Accept Terms and Conditions
   - Click "Create Account"

5. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click verification link
   - Account activated successfully

#### Registration Tips

- **Email**: Use a valid email address you can access
- **Phone**: Include country code for international numbers
- **Password**: Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols
- **Security**: Don't share your password with anyone

### First Login

#### Login Process

1. **Go to Login Page**
   - Visit https://vebstore.com/login
   - Enter your credentials

2. **Enter Credentials**
   ```
   Email: [Your registered email]
   Password: [Your password]
   ```

3. **Security Options**
   - "Remember Me" - Stay logged in on this device
   - "Show Password" - Toggle password visibility
   - "Forgot Password" - Reset password link

4. **Two-Factor Authentication** (Optional)
   - Enter OTP sent to your phone
   - Use authenticator app code
   - Complete login process

#### Login Troubleshooting

**Common Issues:**
- **Incorrect Password**: Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- **Account Locked**: Wait 30 minutes or contact support
- **Email Not Verified**: Check spam folder for verification email
- **Browser Issues**: Clear cache and cookies
